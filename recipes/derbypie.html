Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 2 eggs beaten
  • 1/2 cup melted butter
  • 2 tablespoons Kentucky bourbon
  • 1 1/2 cup chopped walnuts or pecans
  • 1 1/4 cup semi-sweet chocolate chips
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 ready-made pie crust

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F.
  2. Combine flour and sugar in a mixing bowl.
  3. Add the eggs and butter; mix to combine.
  4. Stir in the bourbon, walnuts, chocolate chips, vanilla, and salt.
  5. Pour the mixture into the unbaked pie crust in a 9 or 9 1/2 inch pie plate.
  6. Bake for 40 to 45 minutes.
  7. Let cool before slicing.
