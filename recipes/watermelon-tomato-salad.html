Watermelon and Tomato Salad

Description

This is a quick and easy summer recipe. You can choose fresh basil, thyme, or mint, but my favorite is thai basil.

Ingredients

  • 5 cups seedless watermelon cubed
  • 1 1/2 pounds tomatoes cubed
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 2 tsp fresh basil, thyme, or mint
  • 1 small red onion quartered and thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup red wine vinegar
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • feta cheese

Steps

  1. Combine watermelon, tomatoes, salt, and sugar. Let stand 15 minutes.
  2. Stir in the remaining ingredients and toss.
  3. Cover and chill 2 hours.
